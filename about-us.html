About Us

The Centre is a not-for-profit incorporated charity situated in Errol Street in the heart of North & West Melbourne.
The Centre has now been in operation for over 45 years and is proudly run by a loyal and committed team of volunteers and paid staff.
Our programs and activities respond to the needs of the North & West Melbourne community, providing an accessible and inclusive agenda which fosters belonging and community spirit, reduces social dislocation and ultimately improves the wellbeing of the community’s members.
The Centre works closely with its community partners and is supported by funding and goodwill from the community, City of Melbourne council, and the Victorian and Federal Governments.
"We are dedicated to providing opportunities for local people to connect learn and share, whatever form this takes. In 2024 we have some great events planned, including Multicultural Eid, Intergenerational Sports programs, the digital expansion of North & West Melbourne News, more short courses aimed at building skills, and the third Queensberry Cup. Together, let's make North & West Melbourne stronger and more connected."

Our Team

North & West Melbourne Neighbourhood Centre is staffed by a dedicated team of professionals across community development, events, project management and administration. We also rely on the goodwill of our volunteer community.
In 2024, we have a team of 12 part-time staff and have recently expanded our team to include bicultural worker specialists.

Our History

A group of volunteers came together in 1975 with the idea of setting up a resource centre providing "several meaningful and necessary functions" in the North Melbourne community. Some of these included providing information, support services to people in need, a centre for people interested in voluntary welfare work, etc.

February 1976 - North Melbourne Neighbourhood Centre opened at the North Melbourne Town Hall as a contact, information and referral centre for the people of North and West Melbourne.

1978/79 - Production of the Community Directory and development of North Melbourne News.

1981 -  Relocated to 56 Errol Street and came to be known as "Centre 56"

1983 -  Relocated to the current premises at 58 Errol Street and came to be known as "Centre 58"

1985 - The first ever street party on Errol street

Late 80s/Early 90s - renamed to North & West Melbourne Neighbourhood Centre

2008 - renamed as The Centre: Connecting Community in North & West Melbourne

2014 - Inaugural Spanish Language Fiesta

2022 - Inaugural Queensberry Cup

2023 - In response to the Centre's Strategic Plan 2022-26 & Community Needs Analysis of 2021, The Centre is renamed as North & West Melbourne Neighbourhood Centre
