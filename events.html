Queensberry Cup

Queensberry Cup is a celebration of North & West Melbourne. Held for the first time in 2022, Queensberry Cup is back this year bigger, better and faster! Up to 50 billycart daredevils will race down Errol Street on contraptions of their own design.

Not just a billycart race, Queensberry Cup is a street festival with something for everyone. A dog show, music from across the globe, The Courthouse Bar, fun activities for kids, community dancers, tasty food and pop-up performances.Enter yourself or your team for a shot at glory and prizes including $1,000 to spend at local shops.

Trackside 1pm to 4pm
The grassy Errol St median strip is the place for local pop up performances between races. Catch mini-sets from The Zahoos, Homebrew Verandah Singers and more.

World Kitchen 1pm to 6pm
The World Kitchen explores food from all corners fo the globe with cooking demonstrations that pay honage to home cooks. Eritrean cuisine, Vietnamese delicacies, bowls on a budget and more.

Plus: Arts House Costuming and Photo booth, Covid vaccinations on-site, Community showcase, Tufting

Kids Zone 1pm to 5pm
Lancashire Lane is a kid's own games alleyway. Cool craft, awesome art activities and a brain-bending book bonanza!

Food & Drink: North Melbourne's newest old pub The Courthouse Hotel are running the Queensberry Corner bar. Hungry tummies will have myriad options from Middle Eastern to Mexican.

For more information, visit queensberrycup.com.au
