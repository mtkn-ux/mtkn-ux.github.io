Community

NWMNC is also involved many other projects that add value to the community. Here are our current other projects:

Assistance & Advocacy

We help our local community by being a source of information, offering assistance, advocating for them and supporting their diverse needs.

Wholefoods Unwrapped Collective

Wholefoods Unwrapped Collective has 400 products from 40 suppliers that are all local, low (or no) waste and low cost. Every week staff volunteers portion their produce from bulk containers into stainless steel Returnr containers. Everything is packed in waste-free, returnable containers. We have collaborated with WUC in this great initiative. If you're interested to buy through WUC, get in touch with us.

Inter-generational Sports

Through a tournament of weekly friendly games of soccer, basketball and volleyball, this project aims to strengthen the relationships between young and middle-aged men living in North Melbourne public housing. The aim is to promote social connections, physical health, and sharing of life experiences between different age groups for mutual benefit.

International Students

We provide opportunities for international students to join in community activities, get work experience, and participate in volunteering activities.

Job Readiness

With the help of skilled and experienced trainers, participants work out why they want to work, what they want from employment, understand their value as a worker to a prospective employer and develop the confidence to present themselves. NWMNC ran with with Judy Lazarus Transition Centre for 12 years and in 2024 are presenting this with The Salvation Army.
