North & West Melbourne News

In its 45th year of publication, The North & West Melbourne News is your local community newspaper.
The N&WM News is published quarterly by NWMNC and is produced entirely by volunteers. The News is hand delivered to households and businesses throughout North and West Melbourne, and copies are also widely available through public places, such as NWMNC and the North Melbourne library.
Community announcements are published free of charge. N&WM News has more than 7,000 copies in circulation.

Our new podcast – "Market to Melrose"

Market to Melrose is a new community podcast by local resident Tom Rigby bringing the voices of North and West Melbourne to Spotify, YouTube or wherever you normally listen.
Each episode of Market to Melrose includes a feature interview based on a story from the News. The podcast has a community noticeboard section for coming local events and achievements, and will soon include advertising for local businesses.

How to contribute

Our community newspaper is a reflection of the diverse voices and talents that make our neighbourhood vibrant.

How much to write

Each contribution is unique so no definitive word length is imposed, but stories are always more effective when trimmed back. A (very) rough guide: usually 300-500 words or thereabouts work well longer stories (features, profiles) might carry a bit more (500-800). If you have any queries, please ask us.

Submitting pictures

Please send us an interesting pic if you can with details of the photographer and any important details about the picture.

Copy deadlines

Please try to keep to our deadlines. If you run into trouble, tell us and we will work something out.
Copy deadline for each quarterly edition is 29 days before publication
