Get Involved

You can get involved with the local community through various ways, like volunteering, teaching, donating, sponsoring, etc.

Volunteering

Our vibrant programs thrive with the help of over 200 dedicated volunteers. From homework tutors, bus drivers, cooks, on-the-ground staff, performers and artists in community events, North & West Melbourne News newspaper team members or delivery, administration workers and more.We have a role to suit you even if you only have a small amount of time to give.
Learn more

Membership

Be a member of the Centre, and have your say in the future development of our community.
Members enjoy:
- Voting rights
- A 10% discount on all full fee courses (not transferable)
- Invites to exclusive Member Events
Learn more

Student Placement

We offer a range of placement options across various areas, including community support, education, outreach, event management, and more. Work alongside our experienced team and get involved in projects that impact our community directly.
Gain valuable skills, enhance your resume, and build a professional network that will serve you well in your future career.
Learn more

Teach a Class

We'd love to hear from you if you have got an amazing skillset you'd like to share with the community.
Please fill in our application form and we'll get in touch with you.
Learn more

Donate

Your donation, no matter the size, is an investment in a brighter future for our community. Every dollar you donate goes towards initiatives that empower, educate, and uplift those who need it most. Whether you're passionate about education, community development, or simply making a difference, your donation matters.
